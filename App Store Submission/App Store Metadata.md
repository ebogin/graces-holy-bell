## Description (1,681 / 2,000)

Grace's Holy Bell is a prayer timer designed for the way people actually pray. A general-purpose stopwatch works, until a stray tap mid-prayer stops the clock or starts a new lap. Grace's Holy Bell is built from the ground up around prayer, so your timing stays intact and your history stays meaningful.

BUILT FOR PRAYER, NOT ACCIDENTS
Every action uses an intentional slider or a confirmation step. You can hold your phone or rest your wrist during prayer without worrying that a bump will stop the timer or reset a session. Accurate timekeeping is the whole point, and the app is built to protect it.

SEE HOW OFTEN YOU PRAY
Slide PRAY to log a prayer. The app timestamps the moment and starts a live timer counting the interval until your next one. Slide again to log the next prayer and begin a fresh interval. Each session builds a clear, chronological log of when every prayer happened and how long each interval lasted, so you can see the rhythm and frequency of your prayer at a glance.

AMEN ALARM
Turn on an optional silent reminder that vibrates your iPhone or Apple Watch a set interval after your last prayer, from 30 minutes to 2 hours. No jarring sounds, just a quiet nudge.

IPHONE + APPLE WATCH
Log and view your prayers from either device. Your iPhone keeps accurate time even when the Watch screen is off, and the two stay in sync automatically, including after they briefly lose connection.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
There are no accounts and no sign-ups. Your prayer logs and settings stay on your device. We don't track you across other apps or websites.

With a calm, retro look, Grace's Holy Bell stays out of your way, so the focus stays on prayer, not the app.
